Prioritize Capital Preservation

The Foundation of Trading Success

"Successful traders prioritize preserving their capital using a stop-loss with proper position sizing and diversification." - Nexus Futures Trader

Expert trader Joshua emphasizes "always using a stop-loss" and "only risking 1-2% of your account in each trade."

Key Risk Management Strategies

  • Setting stop-loss orders: Predetermined exit points that limit potential losses on each trade
  • Proper position sizing: Calculating trade size based on account size and risk tolerance
  • Hedging techniques: Using options or correlated assets to offset potential losses
  • Monitoring volatility: Adjusting position sizes based on current market conditions
  • Using futures spreads: Trading related contracts to reduce exposure to market-wide moves

The 1-2% Rule

One of the most consistent recommendations across trading literature is to risk only 1-2% of total account value on any single trade. This approach ensures that:

  • A string of consecutive losses won't devastate your account
  • You can remain psychologically stable during drawdowns
  • You have time to learn and improve your strategy

As Joshua noted in his interview, "Survival is the key to becoming a profitable trader." Without proper risk management, traders often blow up their accounts before they can develop proficiency.
