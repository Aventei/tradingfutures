Current State of Knowledge

When most people think about trading, they get it all wrong:

  • "It's basically just gambling with fancier charts"
  • "I'll be rich by next month!"
  • "Just buy when it's low and sell when it's high, right?"
  • "Anyone can do it - how hard can it be?"

These oversimplified ideas are why so many people lose money. Trading isn't simple - it's actually pretty complex and takes real skills to do well. The movies make it look easy, but real trading is a whole different story.

What People Think

  • Easy money while sipping coffee
  • Heart-racing excitement
  • Getting rich in weeks

What It's Actually Like

  • Learning complex strategies
  • Controlling your emotions
  • Protecting your money first
